Counting Change

Posted by Krystel Rae Davis on 2018-01-22
#Miracle Messages, Homeless, ATX, Progress, Austin, Texas

The other day while walking around downtown Austin, I came across a man standing outside of a convenience store, counting change. It’s a simple thing, counting change but based on the man’s dishelved appearance, I assumed he was homeless, and therefore, his counting change was more. Now, several things ran through my head at this moment and the only conclusion I came to was that anything I did was not going to help his situation. I could have done several things to help him in that moment, talk to him, give him more change, buy him a meal, etc. But I want to do more than that. I want him to not have to worry about if he has enough money to buy food or water and have a place to stay. Yes, I know these are just assumptions, he really could have just been counting change to count change. But there really are people out there who need help, people who need more than just change or one meal or a bed in a shelter whenever one is available. I am looking for real answers, real solutions that cannot be solved by one person or one organization.

I’ve been looking into research the last couple days and everything countinues to point to permanent supportive housing.

Hi, I'm Krystel Rae and I want to encourage others to facilitate change.

Let's start something good.
